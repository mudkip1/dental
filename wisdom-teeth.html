Wisdom Teeth

Wisdom teeth are the last teeth in the back of the mouth.

For most people, they do not come out straight.

Poorly positioned wisdom teeth can cause problems in the future like:

tooth infection,

gum infection,

or a large cavity on the tooth next to it.

Wisdom teeth are generally easier to remove at a younger age.

There are also generally less surgical complications.
