Replacing Missing Teeth

There are different ways to replace missing teeth.

An implant tooth feels the most natural since it is the most alike to your natural tooth.

First, an artificial root is placed where the previous root used to be.

Then, a crown is placed on top of the artificial root.

A bridge replaces the missing tooth using the teeth next to it as support.

It stays in the mouth and feels natural.

Extra care is needed to clean the bridge and supporting teeth.

A partial denture can be taken in and out of the mouth. It sits on top of the teeth and gums.

It is usually the more affordable option, but feels the least natural.
