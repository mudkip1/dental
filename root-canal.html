Root Canal

A root canal is needed when the nerve of the tooth is infected or inflammed.

A root canal cleans out the infected areas of the tooth's roots and fills it.

It allows you to keep the tooth as the infection slowly heals.

Often, the tooth needs a crown after because it becomes weaker to chewing and can break apart.

A filling can be placed instead, but the filling or the tooth can break apart.
